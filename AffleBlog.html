Affle (India) Limited (NSE:AFFLE), a leading player in mobile advertisement services and software development, is poised for significant growth as the upcoming festive season in India promises a remarkable surge in online sales. About¶

Affle (India) Limited, together with its subsidiaries, provides mobile advertisement services through information technology and software development services for mobiles in India and internationally. It operates through Consumer Platform and Enterprise Platform segments. The company provides reselling services of advertisement space for online publishing companies; and customized mobile app development services.

Additionally, it is anticipated that loan applications will rise by 40-50%, surpassing 10 lakh applications during the festive season.

Strong Revenue Growth: Affle's total revenue growth of 433% over the past five years, boasting a CAGR of 39.79%, indicates its capacity to capture market share and expand its top-line performance.
